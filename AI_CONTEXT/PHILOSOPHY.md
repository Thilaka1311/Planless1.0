# Planless MVP Product Philosophy, Behavioral & UI Design Rules

This document serves as the official and permanent product philosophy, interaction style guides, UI design laws, and design reference system for the **Planless** application.

Every future screen, component, flow, interaction, and feature generated for Planless must adhere consistently to the constraints, values, and guidelines defined herein.

---

## PRODUCT PHILOSOPHY RULES

1. **Social Coordination Focus**  
   Planless is a social coordination platform built strictly to help real-world cohorts discover, align, and execute real-world plans quickly and effortlessly.

2. **The Plan is the Core Object**  
   The primary building block of Planless is the **Plan**. The core focus is not user posts, creator feeds, public influencer scores, or algorithmic content consumption, but facilitating offline connection.

3. **The Core Loop**  
   Every major interaction in the application revolves seamlessly around:  
   $$\text{Plan} \longrightarrow \text{Experience} \longrightarrow \text{Memory}$$

4. **Friction Elimination**  
   The application exists to reduce the administrative friction between:  
   *“I want to do something spontaneously”* and *“The plan is happening.”*

5. **Real-World Priority**  
   The product prioritizes genuine, real-world interactions over prolonged in-app screen time and engagement farming.

6. **Emotional Tone & Aesthetic Vibe**  
   The look, feel, and wording of the app should be:  
   - **Simple** (uncluttered, low cognitive overhead)  
   - **Social-first** (anchored in trusted friends, campus circles, or cells)  
   - **Lightweight** (fast micro-interactions, swipeable interfaces)  
   - **Calm** (generous spacing, dark eye-safe cards, soft colors)  
   - **Modern & Culturally Real** (honest human copy, zero corporate-talk)

7. **Negative Product Definitions (What Planless is NOT)**  
   Planless is strictly **NOT** a clone of or a placeholder for:  
   - *Discord* (avoid chaotic nested channels or endless text list threads)  
   - *Meetup* / *Eventbrite* (avoid dry, transactional corporate event catalog desks)  
   - *Splitwise* (avoid clinical, heavy expense calculators; keep ledger co-pays lightweight)  
   - *Productivity Tools* / *Kanban Boards* (this is social play, not work calendars)  
   - *Traditional Content Media* (avoid algorithmic feeds, likes, or endless scrolls)

8. **Versatility of Plans**  
   Plans in Planless spans across four dimensions:  
   - **Spontaneous** (e.g., *"spontaneous football match tonight at 8 PM"*)  
   - **Recurring** (e.g., *"weekly Thursday cinema rituals"*)  
   - **Scheduled in Advance** (e.g., *"weekend roadtrip ride"*)  
   - **Culturally/Socially Anticipated** (e.g., *"major Marvel movie release groupup"*)

9. **The 30-Second Mandate**  
   A user must be able to create, configure, publish, or join any active plan in **under 30 seconds** with minimal input keystrokes.

10. **MVP Simplicity Over Feature Depth**  
    Clean layouts, elegant type-pairings, and smooth transitions are far more valuable than half-cooked deep features.

11. **Cognitive Load Reduction**  
    Every view and dialog must aggressively filter extraneous options, numbers, stats, or systems to eliminate decision fatigue.

12. **Aesthetic Inspiration Pillars**  
    - **Instagram profile simplicity**: Clean identity blocks, personal photo highlights, and direct action.  
    - **Spotify-style calmness**: High-contrast modern interface with deep dark canvases, neon peach/orange accents, and pristine typographic hierarchy.  
    - **Opal-inspired spacing**: Rich breathing room, rounded layouts, and soft interaction cards.

13. **Social invitation, not administrative transactions**  
    Joining plans should feel exciting and inviting. Use animated states, active face piles, and micro-interactions rather than dry text receipts.

14. **Metric Zero**  
    The profile and feeds must aggressively avoid:  
    - Follower/following metrics  
    - Vanity scoreboards or public gamification ranks  
    - Influencer profiles or creator-orientated dashboards  
    - Engagement metrics (e.g., "views count")

15. **Coordination Above Consumption**  
    Every pixel must exist to nudge coordination, co-planning, and archiving memories together.

16. **Desktop-First Execution, Mobile-First Constraints**  
    The interface must run fluidly across all container widths but prioritize thumb-friendly touch zones, swipe gesture ergonomics, and bottom navigation safety.

17. **Speed Beats Complexity**  
    Fast feedback loops (e.g., tactile toasts, instant optimistic state joins, swift toggle switches) are vastly superior to bloated complex transition setups.

18. **Warmth of Design**  
    The app uses rich, dark slate backgrounds (`#09090b` / `#0C0C0E`), soft visual cards with rounded corners (`rounded-3xl`), and carefully balanced highlights (`text-[#ff8b66]`) to communicate visual premium.

19. **Real-Life Snapshot Concept**  
    Planless is designed as *"Instagram for real-world spontaneous experiences"* rather than a platform for pure content generation.
